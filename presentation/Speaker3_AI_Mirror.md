# Speaker 3: The AI Mirror & Cognitive Break

**Time:** ~45 seconds

## Script / Talking Points

**[The Manipulation Mirror]**
"Once the scan completes, we show the user the 'Manipulation Mirror.' We clearly label the exact tactic used—in this case, a 'Fear Anchor.' We expose the exact phrases the attacker used to trigger that fear."

**[Gemini AI Explanation]**
"We also pull in Google's Gemini AI to instantly generate a plain-English, jargon-free explanation of the attack, telling the user exactly what to do."

**[Action: Click 'Take 5 Seconds']**
"But here is the most important feature: The 5-Second Cognitive Cooldown. Phishing relies on artificial urgency. By forcing a 5-second timer, we physically break the panic loop, allowing the user's rational brain to catch up."

**[Safe Resolution]**
"Instead of leaving them hanging, we provide a safe redirect directly to the real PayPal website, fulfilling their original need securely."

**[Hand-off]**
"But what happens if they already clicked the link before scanning? [Speaker 4] will explain our post-click strategy."
