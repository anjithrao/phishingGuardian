# Speaker 1: The Problem & Introduction

**Time:** ~30-45 seconds

## Script / Talking Points

**[Hook]**
"Traditional anti-phishing tools have a major flaw: they protect the inbox, but they don't protect the human. They block malicious URLs, but they fail to address the psychological manipulation that attackers use to make us click in the first place."

**[Introduce PhishGuard AI]**
"This is PhishGuard AI. It’s a tool designed not just to detect phishing, but to educate and protect the user at the exact moment of vulnerability."

**[Demo Action]**
*(Click on the "💳 PayPal Suspension" scenario in the UI)*
"Let's look at this PayPal email. It bypassed the spam filter. The user is looking at an email saying their account will be suspended in 24 hours. The natural human reaction is panic."

**[Hand-off]**
"Let's see what happens when the user runs this through PhishGuard. [Speaker 2] will explain the scanning pipeline."
